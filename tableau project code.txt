1. Connect to your HAR dataset.
2. Drag the "Timestamp" field to the Columns shelf.
3. Drag the "Sensor Data (X-axis)" field to the Rows shelf.
4. Drag the "Sensor Data (Y-axis)" field to the Rows shelf.
5. Drag the "Activity" field to the Color or Shape shelf to differentiate data points by activity.
6. Customize the chart's appearance, labels, and tooltips as needed.
7. Save the worksheet and include it in your dashboard.
